Related Work: Multi-Agent RL with Action/Policy Prediction of Other Agents
==========================================================================

  "Opponent Modeling in Deep Reinforcement Learning" (DRON).
  ICML. https://arxiv.org/abs/1609.05559

- Hong, Su, Shann, Chang, Lee (2018).
  "A Deep Policy Inference Q-Network for Multi-Agent Systems" (DPIQN / DRPIQN).

- Raileanu, Denton, Szlam, Fergus (2018).
